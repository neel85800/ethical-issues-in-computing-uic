# The Tortured Child

Suppose you were called upon to rebuild human society so that everyone in the world would be happy and would find peace and tranquility. There is a way to do this, but you know that it requires you to torture just one single creature: a child.

The child must be locked in a small outhouse somewhere and remain in torture and desparation. However, through the child's tears, you can build a society where everyone else is happy.

_Would you agree to do set up society this way?_
